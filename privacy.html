LyricVibe Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

LyricVibe is a desktop music visualizer app that displays animated lyrics and visual effects for music playback.

Information LyricVibe May Use

If a user chooses to connect Amazon Music, LyricVibe may request access to playback metadata such as song title, artist, album, album artwork, playback state, and related playback information.

How Information Is Used

Playback metadata is used only to display lyrics, album artwork, and animated visual effects inside the LyricVibe desktop app.

Local Storage

LyricVibe stores app settings locally on the user's device, such as theme preferences, visualizer settings, and connection preferences.

Data Sharing

LyricVibe does not sell personal data. LyricVibe does not intentionally collect payment information, passwords, private messages, or unrelated personal data.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: Private personal-use app. No public support contact.
